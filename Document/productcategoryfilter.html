JMS Product Filter

Information

JMS Product filter show products following tab with chosen type (Featured Product,New Product,Best Seller,On Sale Product ) in backend


Configuration

Go to Modules > Modules > JMS Product Filter, click Configure
  • 1Number of Columns: Number of Columns to Show
  • 2Number of Rows : Number of Rows To show
  • 3Totals number of product : total of products will load from database
  • 4Features products: Yes/No
  • 5New Products: Yes/No
  • 6TopSeller Products: Yes/No
  • 7Special Products: Yes/No
  • 8OnSale Products: Yes/No
  • 9Scroll per page: Yes/No
  • 10Display products' prices: Yes/No
  • 11Category : Choose Categories to Display
