JMS Hot Deals

Information

JMS Hot Deals show slider of sale products at homepage


Configuration

Go to Modules > Modules > JMS Hot Deals, click Configure
  • 1Number of Product Show : number products will load
  • 2Speed : speed of moving
  • 3Auto Play : Yes/No
  • 4Navigation: Yes/No
  • 5Pagination: Yes/No
  • 6RewindNav : Yes/No
  • 7ScrollPerPage: Yes/No
  • 8Views all product : Yes/No


Management products

  • 1Add new button.
  • 2Enable/disable button.
  • 3Edit button.
  • 4Delete button.


Adding hot deal product

  • 1Type for searching product.
  • 2Set expired time for product.
  • 3Active.
