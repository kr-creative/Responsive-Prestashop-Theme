Featured products on the homepage

Information

Featured products on the homepage module show featured products at homepage


Configuration

Go to Modules > Modules > Homefeatured Product, click Configure
  • 1Number of Products Per Column: Number of Products Per Column
  • 2Number of Products Per Row : Number of Products Per Row
  • 3Auto Play : Number of product per col
  • 4Number of products to be displayed : Number of products to be displayed.
  • 5Randomly display featured products : Enable if you wish the products to be displayed randomly (default: no).
